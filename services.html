The Customer Reactivation Engine

Your best source of new revenue is sitting in a spreadsheet you haven’t opened in two years.

Most local service businesses have hundreds — sometimes thousands — of past customers who already trust them. They’ve already paid once. They already know your work. And they’ve gone completely quiet not because they chose a competitor, but because nobody reached back out.

The Customer Reactivation Engine changes that. We take your existing customer data, clean and organize it into a working pipeline, and deploy a personalized multi-channel outreach campaign engineered to turn dormant names into live bookings — fast.

What we deliver

  1. Clean, format, and centralize your past customer and lead data into a master pipeline database
  2. Deploy 2–3 personalized text and email follow-up sequences with a compelling offer designed to generate immediate engagement
  3. Set up internal routing so every hot reply lands back with you instantly — via text notification or an automated calendar booking link

Timeline

7–14 days from data delivery to campaign launch

Result

A surge of booked appointments from people who already trust you — typically within 72 hours of launch.

Investment

$1,500 one-time setup, plus a percentage of revenue generated or a flat $500 performance bonus

The Instant Responder System

The lead that doesn’t get a response in the first five minutes is usually gone by the time you call back.

Speed-to-response is the single highest-impact conversion variable in local service. It isn’t about being the best — it’s about being the first voice a new lead hears. Most businesses respond to new inquiries hours later, if at all. We automate that response to happen in under 90 seconds, around the clock.

The Instant Responder System unifies every inbound channel you have — Google Business Profile, your website, Facebook and Instagram, paid ads — into one centralized framework. The moment someone reaches out, they get a professional, human-grade reply. Automatically. While you’re on a job, asleep, or off the clock.

What we deliver

  1. Unify all inbound channels (Google Business Profile, paid ads, website forms, Facebook and Instagram DMs) into one single inbox framework
  2. Automated text and email response sequences that engage new leads within 90 seconds — 24/7
  3. A full 5-day follow-up sequence that runs automatically until the lead books, requests work, or opts out
  4. A simple, brand-aligned lead-capture web page wired natively into your pipeline backend

Timeline

14–21 days to configure, test, and go live

Result

Zero missed inbound opportunities. An immediate, measurable lift in live bookings and scheduled jobs.

Investment

$2,000 setup, plus $497–$997 / month management (scaled by inbound data volume)
