Service 01

The Customer Reactivation Engine

Your best source of new revenue is sitting in a spreadsheet you haven’t opened in two years.

The Customer Reactivation Engine changes that. We take your existing customer data, clean and organize it into a working pipeline, and deploy a personalized multi-channel outreach campaign engineered to turn dormant names into live bookings — fast.

What we deliver

  • Clean, format, and centralize your past customer and lead data into a master pipeline database
  • Deploy personalized text and email follow-up sequences with compelling offers designed to generate immediate engagement
  • Set up internal routing so every positive response lands directly back with your team instantly

What you provide

  • A CSV export of your past clients and leads (name, phone, email is all we need)
  • A real offer or incentive to give them a reason to come back

Timeline

7–14 days from data delivery to campaign launch

Result

A surge of booked appointments from people who already trust you — typically within 72 hours of launch.

Service 02

The Instant Responder System

The lead that doesn’t get a response in the first five minutes is usually gone by the time you call back.

Speed-to-response is the single highest-impact conversion variable in local service. It isn’t about being the best — it’s about being the first voice a new lead hears. Most businesses respond to new inquiries hours later, if at all. We automate that response to happen in under 90 seconds, around the clock.

The Instant Responder System unifies every inbound channel you have — Google Maps, your website, social media, paid ads — into one centralized framework. The moment someone reaches out, they get a professional, human-grade reply. Automatically. While you’re on a job, asleep, or off the clock.

What we deliver

  • Unify all inbound communication channels (Google Maps, paid ads, website, social) into one single inbox framework
  • Automated multi-channel response sequences that engage new leads within 90 seconds — 24/7
  • A full 5-day follow-up sequence that runs automatically until the lead books, requests work, or opts out
  • A custom-built, brand-aligned web property optimized strictly for lead capture — wired natively into your pipeline backend

What you provide

  • Admin access to your current web properties, business listings, and social profiles
  • A list of your most common customer FAQs

Timeline

14–21 days to configure, test, and go live

Result

Zero missed inbound opportunities. An immediate, measurable lift in live bookings and scheduled jobs.

Pricing

$2,000 setup
+ $497–$997/month management (scaled by inbound data volume)
