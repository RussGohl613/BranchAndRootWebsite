How It Works

What you do — and what you see come back.

Five steps from first conversation to compounding revenue. Your part is short. Ours is the build.

  2. Approve the Plan We come back with a tailored proposal — which system to start with, what we’ll build, and what to expect. You say yes (or ask questions until you do). Result: a defined scope, timeline, and outcome.
  3. Hand Off the Keys You give us the access we need — your CRM, phone line, Google profile, customer list. That’s it. We handle the build. Result: zero lift on your team; the system goes live without disrupting your day.
  4. Watch It Run Within the first 30 days, dormant customers start booking, after-hours leads stop slipping, and you get a weekly report showing exactly what’s happening. Result: measurable revenue recovered before we ever talk about new traffic.
  5. Scale When Ready Once the foundation is humming, we turn on growth — a custom website, search visibility, and Facebook & Instagram ads — at the pace your business can handle. Result: predictable new customers every week, on top of recovered revenue.
