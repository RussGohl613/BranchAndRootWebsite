Where you are today

Elite work and elite reviews — but hard to find

Mark has built a genuinely rare reputation: a perfect 5.0 on Google across 129 reviews, 4.9 on Yelp, and 25 years of luxury-credible, owner-operated work. The craft isn't the problem. Getting found by the next customer is.

Hard to find on Google

The reviews are stellar, but beyond people who already know the name, OC Dents is tough to surface in the Google map pack and search results where new customers actually look.

A website working against you

We found it during our audit: your homepage HTML has a hidden block of foreign-language online-casino spam links injected into it — the signature of a hacked or compromised WordPress plugin. Google can quietly suppress your rankings (or flag the site "this site may be hacked"), erasing the visibility your reviews should be earning. The good news: it's fixable fast — Phase 1 below cleans it out.

No steady social presence

The before/afters are the kind of content people stop scrolling for — but posting is sporadic. Without a steady cadence, the work that should be building an audience mostly stays on Mark's phone.

No time to shoot every week

Mark is a one-person operation — he's in driveways fixing cars all day. There's simply no room to plan, shoot, edit, and schedule photos and video on top of the real work.

The bottleneck isn't quality or demand — it's visibility and consistency. That's exactly what an AI-powered engine is built to solve, without adding hours to Mark's week.

The core idea

One profile in. Infinite on-brand content out.

Instead of a never-ending content treadmill, we build a single reusable AI profile of OC Dents and Mark — the brand, the voice, the visual style, the services, and the Orange County geography. Captured once, it becomes the source everything else runs from.

  • We encode the brand once. Logo, the orange-on-charcoal palette, the signature reflection-board photography, Mark's honest no-upsell voice, and the full service and city list.
  • AI generates on demand. On-brand before/afters, reels, Google posts, and ad creative — produced from the profile whenever we need them.
  • It always sounds like Mark. Because the voice and rules live in the profile, every caption and ad reads like him — never generic, never corporate.

What the engine drives

Four pillars, one source of truth

The profile feeds four channels that work together — each one designed to put OC Dents in front of more of the right Orange County drivers, and turn the work Mark already does into compounding visibility.

01
Get found on the map

Google Business Profile

Your strongest near-term lever. We turn a good listing into a local-search magnet so OC Dents shows up when people search "dent repair near me."

  • Full profile optimization — categories, services, Q&A, attributes
  • Weekly AI-written GBP posts that keep the listing active
  • A review-growth system to keep the 5.0 momentum building
  • Geo-tagged photos and a clean, consistent service list

The outcome: rank higher in the Google map pack and capture more "near me" searches across OC.

02
Be the answer everywhere

SEO / AEO / GEO

We make OC Dents the obvious answer in classic search and in the new AI-powered ones — so the brand gets surfaced and cited whether someone Googles it or asks an assistant.

AEO = Answer Engine Optimization (being the answer in voice and featured snippets). GEO = Generative Engine Optimization (getting cited by AI search like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews).

  • Service × city landing pages (e.g. "PDR in Irvine")
  • LocalBusiness / AutoRepair schema so engines read the facts
  • FAQ content tuned for answer engines and voice search
  • Consistent NAP and entity data across the web

The outcome: get found in Google and cited by AI search — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews.

03
Build an audience

Organic social

A steady Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook cadence generated straight from the profile — the scroll-stopping before/afters and explainers that show off the craft, posted consistently.

  • Before/after reels that prove the paintless promise
  • PDR explainers, ASMR rod-work, and myth-busting posts
  • Captions in Mark's honest, plain-spoken voice
  • A reliable schedule — no daily shoots required

The outcome: a growing local audience and a feed that sells the work for you, around the clock.

04
Reach more, faster

Paid ads

When you want to accelerate, we run Meta and Google ads built from the same profile — geo-targeted across Orange County and refreshed continuously, so creative never goes stale.

  • Meta + Google creative variants from the profile
  • Geo-targeted to your real service area across OC
  • Fresh creative on tap — no new photoshoots
  • Continuously tested and optimized for cost-per-lead

The outcome: a controllable tap of new dent-repair leads, dialed up or down whenever you choose.
