Privacy Policy

Last updated June 17, 2026

Overview

Appliance Care of San Clemente ("Appliance Care," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our site you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit our callback or contact form, we collect the name, phone number, and any repair details you choose to provide.
  • Automatically collected information. Like most websites, we may collect standard technical data such as your browser type, device, approximate location, and pages viewed, through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your request and schedule an appliance repair or service.
  • To contact you by phone, text, or email about your repair.
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or misuse.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand site traffic and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work as intended if you do.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate our business (for example, form processing, hosting, or analytics), and only as needed to perform those services, or where required by law.

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it.
  • Request access to or deletion of your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information — though, as noted above, we do not sell your personal information.

You will not be discriminated against for exercising any of these rights. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Data Retention & Security

We keep your information only as long as needed to respond to your request and meet legal requirements, and we take reasonable measures to protect it. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, however, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your information? Reach Appliance Care of San Clemente at (949) 492-1225 or 112 E Avenida Ramona, San Clemente, CA 92672. You may also use the contact form on our website.
