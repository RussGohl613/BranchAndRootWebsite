Business Overview

Di Rock General Contracting & Roofing is a family-owned roofing and general contracting firm based at 28562 Oso Pkwy, Ste D206, Rancho Santa Margarita, CA 92688, owned and operated by Mark Di Rocco (legal name Mark Gregory Di Rocco) and reachable at (949) 545-1976. Branch & Root Recommendation

Recommended Service Tier
Website + SEO / AEO + Meta Ads

Why this tier. Di Rock already has the two hardest assets to build — a functional, owner-forward WordPress / Elementor site and a genuinely elite reputation (4.9 Angi / 5.0 Yelp). What it lacks is everything that turns that reputation into inbound volume:

  • No above-the-fold lead form and no email capture — demand leaks to an off-site Jobber link
  • No blog / AEO content to win organic and answer-engine discovery
  • No confirmed Google Business Profile — invisible in the local map pack
  • No paid traffic, while competitors (South County 150+, Pacific 54, Monarch 72) win on review volume and local-SEO depth

A combined Website + SEO/AEO + Meta Ads engagement is the highest-leverage path: fix conversion on the site, claim and build the GBP, publish AEO content to close the local-SEO gap, and layer Meta Ads targeting RSM homeowners to drive immediate volume against a 5-star reputation that already closes.

Estimated revenue opportunity

A claimed Google Business Profile plus an above-the-fold lead form and Meta Ads targeting RSM homeowners could realistically convert this 5.0-star reputation into an additional 4–8 qualified roofing leads per month — at typical South-OC re-roof tickets, that represents a five-to-low-six-figure annual revenue lift from demand the brand is currently failing to capture.
