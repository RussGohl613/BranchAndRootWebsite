Business Overview

RSM Lock & Safe is an owner-operated locksmith based in Rancho Santa Margarita, California, run by Bob Slama (Robert Slama) from 14 San Ricardo, Rancho Santa Margarita, CA 92688 and reachable at (949) 589-1957. The business has operated for 38 years (since 1987), giving it one of the longest local track records in its market. Its service line covers both residential and commercial work — deadbolt installation, lock rekeying, keyless-entry deadbolts, door repair, mailbox locks, panic bars, sliding-door locks, and residential and commercial lockouts.

Where RSM leads vs. lags

Leads: RSM Lock & Safe leads on the things that are hardest to fake: a genuinely 38-year local track record, a named owner-operator (Bob Slama) who personally does the work, a strong 4.5★ Yelp base across 62 reviews, and a fast-response reputation — credibility a newer competitor cannot buy.

Lags: Where it lags is entirely fixable presentation and coverage: it trails Ben’z badly on review volume (62 vs. 346) and Google visibility (0 vs. an active profile), and it is structurally narrower — weekday-only hours, no 24/7 or after-hours coverage, and no automotive/car-key service, all of which Ben’z, Leonard, and RSM Lake advertise. It also displays no license/“Licensed & Bonded” badge, while Leonard makes its license a headline. The strategic read: RSM is the trusted incumbent that is simply under-marketed — competitors are winning on availability claims and discoverability, not on reputation.

Branch & Root Recommendation

Recommended Service Tier
The Foundation Bundle

Website rebuild + Instant Responder

Why this tier. The current site is a contentless GoDaddy single-pager — no services, no phone, no address, no real CTA, and no trust signals — sitting on top of a credibly verified 38-year local brand with a 4.5★ / 62-review Yelp base. That is a strong reputation trapped behind a weak storefront. The Foundation Bundle fixes exactly that gap: a proper website rebuild (services, trust signals, a license badge once confirmed, click-to-call) plus an Instant Responder to capture the lockout demand currently bleeding to 24/7 competitors like Ben’z (346 reviews). It converts an established, word-of-mouth reputation into discoverable, bookable jobs without asking Bob to change how he runs the trade.

Estimated revenue opportunity

Recovering even a handful of jobs per month that today go to 24/7 competitors — by making RSM discoverable on Google and instantly responsive to after-hours lockout inquiries — would, at typical locksmith ticket values, return the Foundation Bundle’s cost within the first one to two months and compound from there as the rebuilt site lifts conversion on its existing 4.5★ reputation.
