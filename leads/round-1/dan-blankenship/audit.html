Methodology & Disclaimer

Data sourcing: This report was prepared from publicly accessible information — specifically, the confirmed existence of active profiles on Zillow (zillow.com/profile/dansellsoc) and Homes.com (homes.com/real-estate-agents/dan-blankenship/e0dm8kq/). Both platforms deploy WAF/CDN security that blocked programmatic access to profile content (CloudFront on Zillow returned HTTP 403; Akamai on Homes.com). A live re-check in June 2026 returned the same 403. Items marked "Not found" / "Unverified" reflect this access limitation and are not negative judgments about Dan's actual profile quality.

Review data: No Zillow or Google review count or rating could be confirmed on two independent sources. Review sentiment themes were drawn from search-result snippets and are tagged Medium confidence. No review counts, ratings, license numbers, transaction counts, or testimonials have been invented. All are flagged for confirmation via the questionnaire.

Framework scoring: The preliminary 28/100 is based on observable confirmation points — two active platform profiles (partial credit) and no confirmed website or GBP. It is a starting-line indicator. Comparable agents with 2–3 years of Zillow activity typically score 45–65 after a completed questionnaire.

Benchmarks: Competitive tiers are based on publicly reported industry data for South-California residential agents and are directional targets, not market-specific guarantees. OC is heterogeneous — luxury coastal markets (Newport, Laguna) carry higher baselines than inland communities.
