Privacy Policy

Last updated June 17, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how HHS Plumbing (“we,” “us”) handles information when you visit this website or contact us about plumbing services.

2. Information we collect

Details you give us. When you submit our contact form or call, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and the details of your request.

Basic usage data. Like most websites, we may collect standard technical information such as the pages you view, your approximate location, and your device and browser type through standard web analytics.

3. How we use your information

To respond to your inquiry and prepare a quote; to schedule and provide plumbing services; to improve our website and service; and to meet legal or regulatory obligations.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate this site and our business (for example, form processing and analytics providers), under appropriate confidentiality obligations, or where required by law.

5. Cookies & analytics

This site may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your California privacy rights

If you are a California resident, you may request access to or deletion of the personal information we hold about you, and you may opt out of any “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell your personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Data retention & security

We keep personal information only as long as needed to respond to your request and meet legal obligations, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it.

8. Children’s privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “last updated” date above reflects the most recent version.
