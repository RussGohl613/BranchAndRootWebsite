Methodology & Disclaimer

Data sourcing: This report draws on publicly accessible information: Dan's live personal website (danblankenshiphome.com), the California DRE public license record for #01343188 (which confirms his salesperson license status, 2002 issue era, and responsible broker), and active profiles on Zillow (zillow.com/profile/dansellsoc) and Homes.com. The license number, phone, email, brokerage, and tenure are cross-confirmed between the website and the DRE record and are marked High. Zillow and Homes.com deploy WAF/CDN security (CloudFront returned HTTP 403; Akamai on Homes.com) that still blocks programmatic access to review counts and ratings — items marked "Not found" / "Unverified" reflect that limitation, not a negative judgment.

Review data: No Zillow or Google review count or rating could be confirmed on two independent sources. Review sentiment themes were drawn from search-result snippets and are tagged Medium confidence. No review counts, ratings, license numbers, transaction counts, or testimonials have been invented. All are flagged for confirmation via the questionnaire.

Framework scoring: The preliminary 42/100 is based on observable confirmation points — a live (if template-stage) personal website, an active DRE license since 2002, a verified brokerage, and two active platform profiles — net of no confirmed GBP, review counts, or production volume. It is a starting-line indicator; an earlier read of 28 predated locating the personal site and DRE record. Comparable established agents typically score 55–70 after a completed questionnaire and a converted site.

Benchmarks: Competitive tiers are based on publicly reported industry data for South-California residential agents and are directional targets, not market-specific guarantees. OC is heterogeneous — luxury coastal markets (Newport, Laguna) carry higher baselines than inland communities.
