## Prompt

Design a complete, modern homepage for **Everlasting Renovations Inc.**, a Christian family-owned remodeling and painting company in Lake Forest, CA (Orange County). Owner: Matthew. Licensed CSLB #1097824. 3-year written warranty. Est. 2015. Phone: (714) 745-2777.

**Brand palette:**
- Primary: #662A08 (burnt orange / deep rust — CTAs, buttons, accents)
- Secondary: #2B5672 (dark steel blue — supporting elements)
- White #FFFFFF background, black body text
- Warm, trustworthy, clean

**Typography:** Work Sans for all text (match their existing Wix branding)

**Tone:** Warm, professional, Christian-family-values. NOT religious-preachy. Tagline-level callbacks ("Family. Faith. Craftsmanship.") only. Trustworthy, detail-oriented. "Not the cheapest — the best."

## Design notes for Stitch
- Mobile-first responsive
- Use real OC/California-feeling imagery for placeholders (craftsman home exterior, modern kitchen, bright bathroom)
- Slight warm shadow on cards (not pure black — try rgba(102,42,8,0.08))
- Rounded corners: 10–14px
- No stock-photo people unless very natural/on-the-job
- The 3D Visualizer section (Section 4) is a concept mockup — show it as a polished UI card, not a literal working 3D scene
