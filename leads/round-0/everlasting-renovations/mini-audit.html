A simple embedded form with SMS notification turns the website into a 24/7 estimate machine.

2 Review Volume 10× Below Market Leader

Xenak Painting & Remodeling shows 329 Yelp reviews against Everlasting’s 28. When homeowners comparison-shop contractors on Yelp or Google, they default to the louder voice — even when quality is equal or lower.

A 3-year written warranty is a genuine market differentiator — none of the three main local competitors offer it publicly. Right now it’s mentioned once in small print on the Wix site instead of anchoring every page above the fold.

Putting the warranty badge above the fold drives conversion without any new ad spend.
