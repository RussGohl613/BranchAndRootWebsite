Methodology & Disclaimer

Data sourcing: This audit was prepared using publicly accessible information — specifically, the confirmed existence of active profiles on Zillow (zillow.com/profile/dansellsoc) and Homes.com (homes.com/real-estate-agents/dan-blankenship/e0dm8kq/). Both platforms deploy WAF/CDN security that blocked programmatic access to profile content (Akamai on Homes.com, CloudFront on Zillow). Scores marked "Unverified" reflect this limitation and are not negative judgments about Dan's current profile quality.

Framework scoring: The preliminary framework score of 28/100 is based on observable confirmation points: two active platform profiles (partial credit per the completeness caveats above) and no confirmed personal website or Google Business Profile. This score is a starting-line indicator, not a final assessment. Comparable agents starting a website project with 2–3 years of Zillow activity typically score 45–65 after a full questionnaire audit.

Benchmarks: Competitive benchmarks are based on publicly reported industry data for Southern California residential real estate agents and should be treated as directional targets rather than market-specific guarantees. OC is a heterogeneous market — luxury coastal markets in Newport Beach and Laguna Beach have higher baselines than inland communities.
